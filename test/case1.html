BriansClub

High Impact Fraud Ongoing Investigation

Case Summary

BriansClub was a major carding site that sold stolen credit card information before being shut down by U.S. authorities. It trafficked millions of credit cards on the dark web, causing widespread financial damage before its seizure.

Operational Details

This site was seized by the Federal Bureau of Investigation and U.S. Secret Service. The operation involved extensive international coordination and undercover purchases to gather evidence.

Legal Proceedings

Charges Filed: Credit card fraud, identity theft, money laundering

Status: Ongoing investigation with multiple suspects under surveillance.
